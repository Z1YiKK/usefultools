The Real Cost of Moving: Data, Strategy & 2026 Price Analysis

📅 May 18, 2026  ·  ⏱️ 6 min read  ·  📚 4 references

The average American moves 11.7 times in their lifetime (U.S. Census Bureau). Yet most people consistently underestimate moving costs by 30-50%. We analyzed industry data to show the real numbers and how to save.

📦 Estimate your move: Free Moving Cost Estimator.

Moving by the Numbers

The American Moving & Storage Association (AMSA) reports 2025 average costs: local move (within 50 miles): $1,250; long-distance (1,000+ miles): $4,890; cross-country full-service (4-bedroom): $12,000+. These are averages — peak-season (May-September) adds 20-30%.

📖 American Moving & Storage Association (2025). Annual Moving Industry Report. Moving.org

Where the Money Goes

A typical full-service move breaks down as: labor (50-60%), transportation (15-20%), packing materials (10%), insurance/valuation (5-10%), and miscellaneous fees (stairs, long carry, elevator, etc.). The single biggest variable is weight — decluttering before moving can save $500-2,000.

📖 U.S. Department of Transportation (2024). Your Rights and Responsibilities When You Move. FMCSA.dot.gov

The Hidden Fee Problem

A 2023 Consumer Reports investigation found that 22% of interstate moves involved charges exceeding the initial estimate by 20%+. Common hidden fees: shuttle service for large trucks ($200-500), stair charges ($75-150/flight), long carry ($100-300), and packing services ($300-1,200). Getting binding estimates is critical.

📖 Consumer Reports (2023). Moving Company Investigation: Hidden Fees and Overcharges. ConsumerReports.org

Cost-Saving Strategies That Actually Work

1. Move mid-week and mid-month (15-25% less). 2. Get 3+ binding estimates. 3. Pack yourself but let movers handle furniture. 4. Use free boxes from grocery/liquor stores. 5. Check if your employer offers relocation (average package: $7,200 for renters, $18,300 for homeowners per Worldwide ERC data).

📖 Worldwide ERC (2025). U.S. Domestic Relocation Policy Survey. WorldwideERC.org
