The Power of Compound Interest

Compound interest is often called the "eighth wonder of the world" — and for good reason. When your interest earns interest, your money grows exponentially over time.

Example: Saving $500/month at 4% APY, it takes just 18 months to reach a $10,000 goal starting from $1,000 — and only about $9,000 is from your own contributions, with the rest coming from interest.
