The Power of Compound Interest

Compound interest is often called the "eighth wonder of the world" — and for good reason. When your interest earns interest, your money grows exponentially over time. The key is starting early and staying consistent.

Example: Saving $500/month at 5% APY compounded monthly, you reach $100,000 in about 14 years — and roughly 30% of that total comes from interest, not your own contributions.
