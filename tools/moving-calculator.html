How Much Does Moving Cost in 2026?

Moving costs have risen significantly. A local 1-bedroom move averages $500–$1,500, while a cross-country 3-bedroom move can cost $5,000–$10,000+ with full service.

Money-Saving Tips

  • Move mid-week or mid-month when demand is lower
  • Declutter before you pack — less stuff = lower cost
  • Get at least 3 quotes and compare
  • Check if your employer offers relocation assistance
