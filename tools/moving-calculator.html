How the Estimate Works

Based on AMSA (2025) industry data, Consumer Reports pricing analysis, and regional labor rate indexes. Actual costs vary by season (peak: May-September, +20-30%), day of week (weekends cost more), and local market conditions.

  • Get 3 binding estimates — non-binding quotes routinely end up 20%+ higher than the estimate (Consumer Reports, 2023).
  • Move mid-week, mid-month — saves 15-25% vs. weekend end-of-month (AMSA data).
  • Declutter before packing: Reducing your load by 10-15% saves $300-500 on a typical move.
