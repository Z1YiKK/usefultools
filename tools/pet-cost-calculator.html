How Costs Are Estimated

Based on APPA 2025 National Pet Owners Survey and AVMA veterinary pricing data. Food costs reflect U.S. market averages for each tier. Regional multipliers adjust for local pricing (vet care and supplies vary 2-3x between metro areas, per Rover.com 2024 data). Emergency fund: 30% of dogs and 20% of cats need at least one major unplanned expense (NAPHIA 2025).
