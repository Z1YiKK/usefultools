The True Cost of Owning a Pet in 2026

Bringing a pet into your home is one of life's greatest joys — but many new pet owners underestimate the real financial commitment. Our pet cost calculator gives you a clear, honest picture of what to expect.

What's Included in the Cost?

  • One-time costs: Adoption/buying fee, spay/neuter, initial supplies (crate, bed, bowls, leash, litter box)
  • Monthly food: Varies by pet size and food quality — from $20/month for small pets to $200+ for large dogs on raw diets
  • Veterinary care: Annual checkups, vaccinations, flea/tick prevention, dental cleaning, and emergency fund
  • Grooming & supplies: Professional grooming, toys, treats, waste bags, litter, bedding replacement
  • Pet insurance: Accident & illness coverage — increasingly essential as vet costs rise

Example: A medium-sized dog in the US costs approximately $1,500–$4,000 per year, or $18,000–$48,000 over a 12-year lifetime. Use our calculator above to get your personalized estimate!
