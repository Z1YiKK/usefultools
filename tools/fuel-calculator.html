How to Save on Fuel Costs

Gas prices fluctuate constantly. Here are proven ways to reduce your fuel spending:

  • Drive smoothly: Aggressive acceleration and braking can reduce MPG by 15-30%
  • Check tire pressure: Under-inflated tires reduce fuel efficiency by up to 3%
  • Use gas apps: Apps like GasBuddy can save you $0.10-$0.30 per gallon
  • Combine trips: A warm engine is more efficient than a cold start
