Fuel Economy Tips

  • Drive smoothly: Aggressive acceleration and hard braking reduce MPG by 15-30% (AAA, 2024).
  • Check tire pressure monthly: Under-inflated tires reduce efficiency by ~3% (U.S. DOE, 2025).
  • Use cruise control on highways: Maintains steady speed, saves 5-10% on long drives.
  • Remove roof racks when unused: Adds aerodynamic drag, reducing MPG by 2-8%.
  • Combine short trips: A warm engine runs more efficiently than multiple cold starts.
