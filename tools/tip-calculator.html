Tipping Around the World

Tipping customs vary widely by country. Here's a quick guide:

  • United States: 15-20% is standard at restaurants
  • United Kingdom: 10-12.5% service charge often included
  • Japan: Tipping is not customary and can be considered rude
  • France: Service is included ("service compris"), but leaving small change is polite
